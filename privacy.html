Privacy Policy

Welcome to Grab Tutorials! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can manage your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from users:

  • User ID (a unique identifier assigned to you by Meta when you log in) to connect your account.
  • Public profile information (such as your name and profile picture) if you log in via Meta.
  • Your user age group to check whether you are under the age of 13.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your login and create your account.
  • Display and personalize your experience with your profile name and picture.
  • Enforce age restrictions by verifying your age group before allowing account creation.

3. How to Request Data Deletion

You have the right to request that we delete any personal data we have collected about you. If you wish to delete your data, please contact us at:

Email: support@grab-tutorials.live

We will process your request within 30 days of receiving it.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as Meta login) that collect information according to their own privacy policies. Please review their policies separately.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
